Mountain Rafting

Purpose: To spark joy and spark the spirit of adventure by plunging into the wild, wonderful world of rafting. Mission: Our mission is to create legendary rafting routes that take you to breathtaking rapids, reveal the magic of rivers, and leave you with stories to tell for a lifetime. We’re all about safety, epic fun, and protecting of our stunning waterways. Join us, and let the river be your playground! Ready to hit the rapids?

History

In 2024, Captain Splash took his first leap off a waterfall and knew he had to share the thrill. With old rafts and a dream, Mountain Rafting was born.

We’ve been making waves ever since, navigating wild rapids surounded by mountains. Our passion for adventure and nature has only grown. Join us and become part of the legacy!
